Description

本次问卷调查旨在研究不同的普通话使用者在面对同样的句子的理解上是否存 在偏差，完成调查问卷时间大约为 3 分钟。在开始完成问卷前，请您阅读以下的实 验须知：

The purpose of this questionnaire is to investigate whether or not different Mandarin speakers have bias in understanding the same sentence. It takes about 3 minutes to complete the questionnaire. Please read the following instructions before completing the questionnaire:

  • 在问卷开头，您的一些个人信息将会被采集（年龄，地区，受教育程度，所 掌握外语种类等）。所有采集到的您的个人信息均会匿名处理并且只会用于对您的数 据结果进行分析，您的姓名及身份资料不会在任何刊物上被提及。

    At the beginning of the questionnaire, some of your personal information will be collected (age, region, education level, foreign language proficiency, etc.). All personal information collected will be processed anonymously and used only for the analysis of your data results. Your name and identity will not be mentioned in any publication.

  • 你所提供的资料可能会在现时的研究项目结束后保留，以供日后研究语言之 用。

    The information provided by you may be retained after the completion of the current research project for future research purposes.

Tasks

You will be asked to complete the following tasks:

  1. Background questionnaire
  2. Read the sentence and answer the questions

Compensation

You will receive 30 Euros for completing all tasks: 10 dollars immediately after completing two language use logs and 20 dollars immediately after completing ALL TWO sessions.

Benefits and Risks

By participating in this research, you will be able to understand how different test types demand different kinds of language processing. Also, your participation will contribute to the field of second language acquisition, which investigates how language is processed and acquired. There are no known risks associated with participating in this study.

Your rights to participate, say no, or withdraw

您可以在数据收集会议结束前的任何时间退出研究，在这种情况下，您的所 有数据将被销毁。

You may withdraw from the study at any time prior to the end of the data collection, in which case all of your data will be destroyed
